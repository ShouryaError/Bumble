Bumble Web: The Bumble Experience, Without Your Phone!

Sometimes, you need a break from staring at your phone screen. Or you might simply prefer making first moves from the comfort of your laptop, desktop, or tablet. Good news: we created Bumble Web, with all the features you love on our app, but without the need for a smartphone. Bumble Web is easy to use, too. All it takes is logging into your current Bumble account through Bumble.com. (And head’s up: you can also create a new Bumble account on Bumble Web if you don’t have one already!)

  1. Get started by selecting the ‘Sign In’ option on www.bumble.com
  2. Then, pick your usual sign in method to access your account (Facebook or Phone Number).
  3. You’ll immediately notice all of your existing chats and matches to the left, and your potential new matches to the right.
  4. Looking to meet new potential dates in your area? Use your left/right keyboard arrows (or click the icons at the bottom of the screen) to go through potential matches in your area.
  5. See a profile that catches your eye? Use the up/down keyboard arrows (or click the up and down arrows at the top and bottom of the profile) to go through a potential match’s profile to learn more.

    6. We guarantee you’ll love seeing profile photos and bios in such a new, vivid way!
